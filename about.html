Our Story

Established in 2014 BS, BirAmar Singh Secondary School was founded to meet the growing demand for quality education in the region. Located in Birtamode-2 Charpane, Jhapa, our institution has been at the forefront of providing advanced technical education.

Since 2073 BS, we've been offering specialized courses in Computer Engineering from grades 9 through 12, integrating practical training through On-the-Job Training (OJT) phases after each academic year.

Computer Engineering Course

Our 4-year Computer Engineering program is designed to equip students with the necessary skills for the tech industry. The curriculum includes:

  • Grade 10: 3 months OJT
  • Grade 11: 3 months OJT
  • Grade 12: 6 months OJT (post-National Examination Board examination)
